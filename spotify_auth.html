🎵 Spotify Authorization for JARVIS

Spotify requires OAuth2 authentication. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Enter your Spotify App details

Get these from Spotify Developer Dashboard

Add this Redirect URI in your Spotify app settings:
http://127.0.0.1:3000/api/spotify/callback

Step 2: Authorize

This opens Spotify in a new tab. Login and approve.

Step 3: Get the Code ⚠️ CODE EXPIRES IN 10 MINUTES!

After you approve, Spotify will try to redirect to localhost and show an error. Look at the URL - copy everything after code= (stop before & or the end)

⚡ IMPORTANT: Complete this step within 10 minutes or the code will expire!
