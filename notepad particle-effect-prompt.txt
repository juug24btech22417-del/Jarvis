Visual:

Pure black background, fullscreen
200–300 small glowing particles forming a nebula/galaxy cluster in the center of the screen
Particles are cyan and electric blue (#00FFFF, #00BFFF, #FFFFFF for the hot core), with a soft glow/bloom effect
Particles have slight random drift at all times — like floating dust in zero gravity

States & Animations:
IDLE state:

Particles float loosely and slowly around the center in a soft cloud shape
Subtle random movement, low velocity, dim glow

ACTIVATED state (triggered by clap / loud sound):

Detect a sharp audio transient via the microphone (Web Audio API or pyaudio)
On clap detection: particles rapidly collapse inward toward the center forming a tight bright core (implosion)
Then immediately burst outward in a radial shockwave/starburst explosion
Bright white-cyan flash at the center during burst
Particles then settle back into the nebula cloud shape

SPEAKING state:

Particles pulse outward rhythmically, amplitude tied to the TTS audio output level
Use Web Audio API analyser node on the speech synthesis output to drive particle velocity

Trigger:

Use SpeechSynthesis + SpeechRecognition (Web Speech API) for voice
Clap = two loud transients within 0.8 seconds activates Jarvis
On activation, Jarvis says "Yes?" and enters listening mode

Tech: Single index.html file, vanilla JS + HTML5 Canvas. No external libraries needed. Make it look like an Iron Man-style JARVIS ambient display.
